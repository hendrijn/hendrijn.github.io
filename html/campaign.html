RISE Foundation Campaign

All-Black Library Brochure

RISE Foundation is dedicated to educating their local community in Waynesboro, VA about Black history. In 2020, they created the only all Black library in the United states. Books are either provided by authors, donated through book drives, or bought with fundraised money. This brochure, made with Adobe Illustrator, serves as a means to educate people about the library.

After-School Program

RISE Foundation Also holds an after-school program for students. I created two deliverables for this. One was a social media post promoting parents to enroll their children in the program. The second was a video PSA also encouraging enrollment. This 30-second clip features b-roll from the program itself.
