Cavapoos

Range from 7-18 lbs

Their size varies but nonetheless will be perfect in small spaces.

Family-Oriented

Cavapoos love to be involved in family events and playing with children.

Need Engagement

Cavapoos do best when given plenty of opportunities to play, walk, or finish complex games.

History

Cavapoos are part poodle part King Charles Cavalier Spaniel. The poodle portion is skilled at water activities and were used in hunting in upper-class families. Cavaliers were bred to be companion dogs of the upper-class (hence being named after the King). The combination first appeared in Australia, and since has become a popular choice as a pet and designer breed.

Care and Upkeep

Cavapoos can have 3 different kinds of fur: hair (terrier look), fleece (loose and wavy), and wool (tight curls). All kinds aren't prone to shedding and require different levels of grooming. Hair coats require little grooming, but fleece and wool coats need more frequent maintenance. They can eat almost anything, so it's important to monitor their diet to prevent weight gain.
